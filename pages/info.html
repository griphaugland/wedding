Wedding Information

Time & Place

The wedding will be held at Canyon Run Ranch, 395 N Rangeline Rd, Pleasant Hill, OH 45359 The ceremony will start at approximately 4:30 PM on Friday, June 30th, 2023. After the ceremony, a reception will be held at the same venue in the old barn. The site of the ceremony is just a short walk from the barn.

Attire

Please wear formal attire. The average high temperature at the venue in June/July is approximately 84°F (29°C), so bear that in mind when choosing your outfit. Wear shoes that will allow you to enjoy a leisurely walk to the woods, where the ceremony will take place. You will regret those spiked heels, but a sturdy pair of wedges would work just fine. There are also golf carts available to shuttle those guests needing a little more assistance to get to and from the ceremony site.

Transportation

The wedding venue is located 75 minutes north of Cincinnati (90 minutes from the Cincinnati Airport.) Guests are welcome to use their own method of transportation to and from the venue, but we will arrange a shuttle to and from the venue for those that do not have another method of transportation. Details about the shuttle departure times will be shared at a later date. Please make sure to indicate in your RSVP whether you wish to ride with the shuttle. The shuttle will return to Cincinnati after the conclusion of the wedding reception.

Food & Drinks

Immediately after the wedding ceremony there will be appetizers and drinks in the Milk Bar (ground floor of the barn) for all guests. When it is time for dinner, we will proceed to the top floor of the barn where our caterer will provide meal options of beef, poultry, and seafood. There will be a free full-service bar at the top floor of the barn. Please make sure to indicate in your RSVP whether you have specific dietary requirements. If you need to add additional information after submitting your RSVP, then please feel free email simon@haugland.com with any questions or to share important information.

Gifting

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift of all. However, if you wish to honor us with a gift, a cash gift would be very welcome.
